Following up on tasks

The core problem

Client Tasks have no assignee by convention, so they never appear in anyone's My Tasks. Asana's entire follow-up system runs on assignee and due date, so unassigned tasks get no automatic nudge to anyone.

Fix optionTrade-off
Add an Internal owner field to every Client Task, separate from assigneeCheapest fix, keeps the Client Task convention intact, one more field to populate
Assign Client Tasks to the RampFuel account owner, keep the "(Client Task)" prefixPuts it in My Tasks automatically, but changes an existing rule, needs Jared and Cole's buy-in
Rule: notify Internal owner if a Client Task has no comment in 7 daysFully native, but only works once Internal owner exists

Status field everywhere

Not started, in progress, waiting on client, blocked, done. Add it to every project, not just Dynasend's, so a board view shows what's stuck at a glance.

Anchor triage to Monday L10

The Monday L10 project already implies a weekly Level 10 meeting. Use it to review overdue and waiting-on-client tasks instead of inventing a new ritual.

Saved searches, pinned

Overdue by client, no update in 10 days, waiting on client. A five-minute setup that replaces manual filter-by-client-field-and-scroll.
